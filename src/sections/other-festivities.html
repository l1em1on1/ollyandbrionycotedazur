The round is likely to cost between 80 – 100 Euros including a buggy (which is compulsory owing to the steep course).

This invite is open to any of the boys/men attending the wedding (sorry girls), please can you let Olly know directly on omyerson@hotmail.co.uk by 15th April if you wish to join.
