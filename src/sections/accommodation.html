Accommodation

Hotels are available in both Ste Maxime & Les Issambres. We would recommend staying in Ste Maxime as there is more to do and is closer to St Tropez should you wish to make a visit.

3* hotels in Sainte Maxime are perfectly fine and you have the choice between ones right in the centre of Sainte Maxime (these don’t have a pool, but you’re right by the beach) or ones that are a 10 minute walk from Sainte Maxime (along the sea) that do have pools, so its up to you what you prefer.

Below is a list of hotels and given the short notice we would recommend booking as soon as possible.

Room rates below are per room for double rooms, and exclude breakfast (normally €10 per day).

Hotel Royal Bon Repos (3*) - www.hotelroyalbonrepos.fr

Rated no. 1 on Trip Advisor, centre of Sainte Maxime, renovated in 2013, all rooms have a balcony with sea view. No pool.

Room rates: €150 - €170

Le Petit Prince (3*) - www.hotel-petit-prince.fr

Centre of Sainte Maxime, superior rooms have balcony with sea view, no pool.

Room rates: €85 - €139

Hotel les Palmiers (3*) - www.hotelespalmiers.com

In the centre of Sainte Maxime, simple hotel, no pool, some rooms have sea view.

Room rates: €94 - €129

Hotel Mas des Oliviers (3*) - www.hotellemasdesoliviers.com

10 min walk into Sainte Maxime (along the beach), pool.

Room rates: €105 - €160

La Belle Aurore (4*) - www.belleaurore.com

Beautiful hotel on the sea, with pool.

Room rates: €165 - €280

Domaine du Calidianus (3*) - www.le-calidianus.com

10 mins walk to Sainte Maxime, lovely hotel with recently renovated rooms, two pools.

Room rates: €137 - €142

Best Western Montfleuri (3*) - www.montfleuri.com

5 mins walk to Sainte Maxime, with pool.

Room rates: €125 - €250

Hotel Matisse (3*) - www.hotel-matisse.com

Renovated in 2013, in the centre of Sainte Maxime with a pool.

Room rates: €110 - €220

Hotel Les Jardins (3*) - www.hotel-lesjardins.com

Bigger than the other hotels, 15 mins walk to Sainte Maxime, large pool.

Room rates: €98 - €158

Jas Neuf Hotel (3*) - www.hotel-jasneuf.com

10 minutes walk to Sainte Maxime, with pool.

Room rates: €86 - €150

Hotel les Santolines (3*) - www.hotel-les-santolines.com

10 minutes walk to Sainte Maxime, with pool. They also have apartments for up to 7 people.

Room rates: €151 - €260

Hotel la Plage (3*) - http://hotel-plage-ste-maxime.com/en/

30 minutes’ walk/5 minutes’ drive to Sainte Maxime, right on the sea (across the road) with a private beach, all rooms have a sea view, no pool.

Room rates: €85 - €125
